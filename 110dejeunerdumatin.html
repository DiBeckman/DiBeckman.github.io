Translation of the Compréhenion et intégration questions about the poem

1. Who are the two characters in the poerm?

2. Who is leaving whom? How do you know?

3. Why is the voice of the narrator so sad?

4. What is the first sign that the man is going to leave?

5. Explain the connection between the weather and the tone of the poem.
