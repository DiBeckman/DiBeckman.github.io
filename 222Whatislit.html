What is literature?
• imaginative writing of recognized artistic value (Shannon, p. 3)

• Literary works do contain levels of meaning that not apparent on first reading. They are just not hidden. (Shannon, p. 13)
• When we interpret a work of literature, we seek meaning important in our own lives rather than the meaning important to the characters in the story. (Shannon, p. 16.)
