WRITING ASSISTANCE RUBRIC
FLF 201 Beckman


Content
Considers: comprehensibility; knowledge of subject; development of ideas; analysis; use of comparison and contrast; relevance to topic assigned.

6 completely understandable, relevant, and thoughtful
4-5 mostly understandable and relevant, needs more supporting detail
2-3 somewhat understandable, limited knowledge and relevance
1 barely understandable; little knowledge and relevance
0 not at all understandable


Grammar (Accuracy)
Considers: complexity of sentence structure, correct use of agreement, number, verb tenses, work order, articles, pronouns, prepositions.

6 all statements were structurally correct
4-5 most statements were structurally correct
2-3 some statements were structurally correct
1 very few statements were structurally correct
0 no statements were structurally correct


Vocabulary
Considers: range of word usage; word and idiom choice, variety; anglicismes.

4 employed a wide variety of vocabulary, accurate word/idiom choice, mastery
3 employed an adequate variety of vocabulary, some errors in word choice
2 employed a limited variety of vocabulary, many errors in word choice
1 employed minimal vocabulary; many translation-based errors
0 inadequate use of French


Organization
Considers: clear statement of ideas; clear beginning, middle, and end; smooth transitions; elaboration of key topics; logical and cohesive sequencing.

4 always coherent and organized
3 generally coherent, some evidence of organization
2 partially coherent, some evidence of organization
1 barely coherent and organized; ideas not well connected
0 incoherent, no organization
