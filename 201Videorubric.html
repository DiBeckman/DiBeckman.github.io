GRADING RUBRIC FOR VIDEO COMMENTARIES

Each video commentary is worth a maximum of 25 points. You can lose points for a commentary that is too long or too short, or is written poorly, not proofread for errors, etc. However, the grade for the video commentary based principally on your comments, feelings, reactions, comparisons of the CULTURAL CONTENT of the video. Do not summarize the film. This activity is meant to have you focus on the culture and observe, compare and comment on what you have learned about France and how it is different from the culture you are most familiar with. If you are not sure what to write about cultural content, the following pages may be helpful: An Introduction to Some Basic Ideas about Intercultural Communication and sample commentaries from my students in Fall 2003.

No late commentaries will be accepted. If you are absent or if you cannot submit the video commentary by the last due date, you must contact the instructor and request an extension. If there is no request and/or no extension, a grade of “0” will be assigned.

24-25 points Commentary contained insightful observations of the cultural content based on details from the video. Comparisons were made between the cultural content of the video and American (or other) culture/history/literature. Student commented on what surprised or intrigued him/her about the cultural content. Student commented on how the cultural content “fit” or did not “fit” with his/her knowledge of the culture.
22-23 points Commentary contained some observations of the cultural content based on details from the video. Commentary contained some of the following: comparisons with another culture/history/literature, comments on what surprised or intrigued him/her about the cultural content, discussion of how this “fit” or did not “fit” with previous ideas about the culture.
20-21 points Commentary contained too much summarization of the video and not enough commentary on the culture. Some cultural comments were made but not enough. May be too short. Some grammar or vocabulary errors.
18-19 points Commentary is all/mostly summary of the video and/or from the commentary, instructor could not tell whether or not the student actually saw the video since the comments are vague and do not relate to the actual cultural content of the video. May be too short. Many grammar or vocabulary errors.
0-17 points Poorly written. Shows little insight. Cultural content not addressed at all.
Student cannot prove he/she saw the video or learned anything from its content. May be too short with excessive margins and/or spacing to make it appear longer.

Useful vocabulary:

J'ai remarqué… ( I noticed…)

J'ai appris que… (I learned that…)

Je ne savais pas que… (I didn't know that…)

J'étais étonné(e)… que + subjonctif (I was surprised that…)

correspondre à (to fit in with)

la vidéo; la culture française, la culture américaine…

trouver + adjectif (je trouve cette vidéo/histoire intéressante /triste/ ennuyeuse/ passionnante/ ridicule)
