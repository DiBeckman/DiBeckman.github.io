Class 27 (Proust) Wednesday 3/23
Print out Lecture notes on Proust, Defamiliarization

Advice about how to read Proust: keep in mind that the first pages are a series of associations made by a person drifting off to sleep; don’t be freaked out by how long the sentences are; don’t stop to wonder what each images means or what is allusion is. Get to the end of the “digression” and you’ll see how it connects thematically with another “digression” chains of association. Read out loud if possible.
