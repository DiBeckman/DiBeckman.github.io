FL 222 Poetry Presentation: Critical Skills

The purpose of this assignment is for you to find an appropriate secondary source, such as an essay, article or portion of a book to use in formulating your personal understanding and interpretations of a poem. You will also become familiar with the Critical Thinking terminology that we will be using in the Thesis Exercise and Term Paper. Even if you are presenting with a partner, each student must choose a different article for this assignment.

Step 1: Identify your resource. Start as soon as you choose your poem, since it may take some time and include a visit to DH Hill. To find an appropriate critical work for your project begin with the Selected Bibliographies in the back of The Norton Anthology (Pages A3-A10). You can also use the Library Tools page to see the many resources available to you from NCSU libraries or chat live with a Reference Librarian. Note: Wikipedia is a common starting point for information about an author or work, but you will need to go to peer-reviewed articles (some may be linked at the bottom of the page) to find appropriate criticism. This page from our Library Tools gives useful guidance. Coordinate with your partner(s), so that each person selects a different article.
