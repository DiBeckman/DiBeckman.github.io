Arrondissement presentations

Paris is divided into 20 districts or arrondissements. Each student will present give a short (2 minute) presentation of one arrondissement during our third class. Create ONE page to represent your arrondissement and add it to the Google Slide Show. Tell about the major cultural/governmental sites (buildings? parks?) in your arrondissement(s) and what we might want to do or see there.
