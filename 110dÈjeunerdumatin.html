Déjeuner du matin by Jacques Prévert

Here the poem read out loud at http://people.ucls.uchicago.edu/~djackso/dejeunerdumatin.htm

Translation of the Compréhenion et intégration questions about the poem

1. Who are the two characters in the poerm?

2. Who is leaving whom? How do you know?

3. Why is the voice of the narrator so sad?

4. What is the first sign that the man is going to leave?

5. Explain the connection between the weather and the tone of the poem.

• Le Message by Jacques Prévert

Hear the poem read out loud at http://www4.ncsu.edu/~dsbeckma/LeMessage.m4a

Translation of the Compréhenion et intégration questions about the poem

1. Imagine this mysterious "someone (quelqu'un)". Is it a man or a woman?

2. How do you imagine this person?

3. Was this person upset when s/he entered the house? Explain.

4. What is the message in the letter, in your opinion?

5. How does this person react to the letter?
