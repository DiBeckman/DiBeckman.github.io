Must-see French films according to Speak the Culture: France (London: Thoroughgood, 2008) with my own additional suggestions after 2006.

There is also a list of films recommended for FLF 110 with synopses, links to trailers and location at NCSU libraries.

For a wikiepedia.fr list of films from 2010-2014 « ayant dépassé le million d'entrée au Box-office, classés ici dans l'ordre du Box-office, see here.

For the list of winners of the Méliès prize (since 1947), see here.

Films in violet are on the FLF 318 syllabus, and films in green are already chosen, so not eligible for class presentations.

Be sure that you can get a copy of the film before you choose it. It would also be a good idea to watch the entire film before making your final choice.
