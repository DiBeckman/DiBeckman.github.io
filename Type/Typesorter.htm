The Keirsey Temperament Sorter

Use the answer form to record your answers.

There are no correct or incorrect answers. This sorter only measures preferences. Decide which choice matches your preference. If someone threw you a ball, would you think about which hand to use? No, because you have a preferred hand. In other words, don't think about how you'd LIKE to be, what answer you'd LIKE to give, but choose the answer that best describes your natural preference. Don't overanalyze the questions.
