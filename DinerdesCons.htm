Le Dîner des Cons


Useful Vocabulary:

The most important word in the film is con, an adjective and noun meaning stupid, idiot, damn fool, imbecile, etc. This is not a word to use in polite conversation. Its usage ranges from very informal to vulgar. Notice the slight difference between the French title: Le Dîner de Cons (The Dinner of Idiots) and the English title, The Dinner Game.
