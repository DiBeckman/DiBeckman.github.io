A bilingual booklet of the texts for the class will be distributed to you in Lille. For your convenience, here are links to the texts online in both English and French.

"The best of all ways to make one's reading valuable is to write about it." Ralph Waldo Emerson

Students will keep a reading journal, taking notes on each text. It will be helpful to answer the following questions:

What is confusing? Qu'est-ce qui n'est pas clair?
What is repeated? Qu'est-ce qui est répété?
What is familiar? Qu'est-ce qui est familier?
What is strange? Qu'est-ce qui est bizarre?
