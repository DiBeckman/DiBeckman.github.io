From the liner notes:
St. Catherine is the patron saint of single girls and the name of Montreal's main street.
This was the first song co-written with Philippe Tatartcheff, a close friend since the sixties, a native of Timmins, a mining town in northern Ontario, formerly a student of French literatuer at La Sorbonne, now a farmer and a poet near Dunham, Québec.
