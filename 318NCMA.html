MUSEUM VISIT

To enhance our appreciation of the arts, you will visit the North Carolina Museum of Art during the semester and write a 2-3 page paper in French about your visit. Choose at least two works to discuss and connect them, if possible with the films we have been studying. You may go on your own or join one of the organized visits listed on the Syllabus on Fridays from 7-8:30: January 30, February 13, and April 17. Email me to sign up for one of the extra-credit guided visits. This assignment is worth 3 homeworks.

Here is a handy thinking routine to use to take notes about what you see, and vocabulary for talking about art in French.

LOOKING: TEN TIMES TWO (A routine for observing and describing)

Look at the image quietly for at least 30 seconds. Let your eyes wander.

List 10 words or phrases about any aspect of the picture.

Repeat Steps 1 & 2: Look at the image again and try to list 10 more words or phrases to your list. How do you say those words and phrases in French?
