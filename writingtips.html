Tips for writing in French

Your paper should be clearly written and well-organized. Refer to your grammar book and a good dictionary as needed. Do not rely on other people or online translators for help; submit only your own original work. See me ahead of time with questions, or for help with corrections.

The most serious writing problems arise when students tries to transform an English sentence word for word into a foreign language equivalent. This frequently happens when students try to express a complex idea in French. It's best to break down a complex idea into a series of simple declarative sentences which represent the facets of its meaning and then rebuild them into a complex or compound sentence which responds to French grammatical rules. Use adverbs, adjectives, conjunctions and other transitional expressions to join your sentences together. Above all, do the outline and drafting of your composition in French rather than English.

As you become more advanced in French, you will find different ways to combine sentences. Here are some examples.

  J'ai un fils. Il a dix ans.
early: J'ai un fils et il a dix ans.
intermediate: J'ai un fils qui a dix ans.
advanced: J'ai un fils âgé de dix ans.

Next, here is a paragraph in two versions--the first at the most elementary level, the second as rewritten by a native speaker. Notice how the native speaker combines sentences, changes word order, and omits words that are repeated too many times. This is something to aim for.

1) Henri est professeur. Il travaille à l'université. Il habite à Paris. Françoise est sa femme. Elle travaille en ville. Ils ont deux enfants. Marie est leur fille. Elle a dix ans. Jean est leur fils. Il a huit ans. Ils ont un appartment. Il est petit. Mais c'est un joli appartement. Il est dans une maison. Cette maison n'est pas grande. Elle n'ets pas petite. Un cinéma est en face.

2) Henri, professeur, travaille à l'université. Il habite Paris avec sa femme Françoise, qui travaille en ville, et leurs deux enfants, Marie, dix ans et Jean, huit. Ils ont un joli appartement situé dans un immeuble de taille moyenne, en face d'un cinéma.
