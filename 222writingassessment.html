WRITING ASSESSMENT RUBRIC FOR SHORT ASSIGNMENTS
FL 222 Beckman

Total score: up to 20 points

Content
Considers: comprehensibility; knowledge of subject; development of ideas; analysis; use of comparison and contrast; relevance to topic assigned.

4 completely understandable, relevant, and thoughtful
3 mostly understandable and relevant, needs more supporting detail
2 somewhat understandable, limited knowledge and relevance
1 barely understandable; little knowledge and relevance
0 not at all understandable


Organization
Considers: clear statement of ideas; clear beginning, middle, and end; smooth transitions; elaboration of key topics; logical and cohesive sequencing.

4 always coherent and organized
3 generally coherent, some evidence of organization
2 partially coherent, some evidence of organization
1 barely coherent and organized; ideas not well connected
0 incoherent, no organization


Vocabulary and Grammar
Considers: range of word usage; word and idiom choice, variety. Complexity of sentence structure, correct usage.

4 employed a wide variety of vocabulary, accurate word/idiom choice, mastery
3 adequate variety of vocabulary, some errors in word choice and grammar
2 limited variety of vocabulary, many errors in word choice, poor grammar
1 employed minimal vocabulary, grammar errors interfere with meaning
0 inadequate

Annotated Copy of the Poem
4 follows guidelines completely; well annotated
3 mostly follows guidelines; adequate annotations
2 somewhat follows guidelines; poor annotations
1 copy of poem submitted with inadequate annotations
0 no annotated copy of poem submitted

Critical Skills Exercise
4 follows guidelines completely; excellent answers and examples
3 mostly follows guidelines; adequate answers and examples
2 somewhat follows guidelines; fair answers and examples
1 does not follow guidelines; poor answers and examples
0 not submitted
