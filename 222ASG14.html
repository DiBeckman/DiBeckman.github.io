A Short Guide, Chapter 14: Poems and Pictures

Links collected by Adam Willson, Spring 2012 Honors Contract Project

  1. W.H Auden, "Musée des Beaux Arts" (about Pieter Brueghel's Landscape with the Fall of Icarus) Poem and painting.
  2. Gwendolyn Brooks, "The Chicago Picasso" (about a Cubist sculpture). Poem and sculpture.
  3. Rita Dove, "The Great Piece of Turf" (about Dürer's painting with this title) Poem and painting.
  4. Robert Fagles, I, Vincent (a book of poems about paintings by van Gogh).
  5. Jane Flanders, "Van Gogh's Bed" (about van Gogh's Vincent's Bed) Painting and poem.
  6. Donald Hall, "Munch's Scream" (About Munch's The Scream) Poem and painting.
  7. Robert Hayden, "Monet's Waterlilies" Poem and 3 of his more than 250 waterlily paintings.
  8. Edward Hirsch, "Edward Hopper and the House by the Railroad" (about Hopper's House by the Railroad) Painting and poem.
  9. Ted Kooser, "Prisoners from the Front" (about Winslow Homer's painting with this title) Painting and poem number 3.
  10. Henry Wadsworth Longfellow, "The Cross of Snow" (About an engraving in a magazine, based on a photograph 1875 by William Henry Jackson of "The Mountain of the Holy Cross") Picture and poem.
  11. Edwin Markham, "The Man with the Hoe" (about Millet's painting with this title) Painting and poem.
  12. Anne Sexton, "The Starry Night" (about van Gogh's The Starry Night) Poem and painting.
  13. John Updike, "Before the Mirror" (about Picasso's Girl Before a Mirror) Painting and poem.
  14. William Carlos Williams, "The Great Figure" (about Charles Demuth's I Saw the Figure Five in Gold) Painting and poem.
  15. William Carlos Williams, "The Dance" (about Brueghel's Peasant Dance) Painting and poem.
  16. Samuel Yellen, "Nighthawks" (about Edward Hopper's painting with this title) Painting and poem.

For additional poem and painting pairs, see The Poet Speaks of Art, a project for English 205 at Emory University.
