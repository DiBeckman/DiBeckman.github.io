Arrondissement presentation:

Paris is divided into 20 districts or arrondissements. Rick Steve's Paris 2010 has a Map Index on page 618 and a tiny map of Paris by arrondissement on page 18. You'll want a good map of Paris by arrondissements handy so you can locate the sites we read about in Paris Paris, Rick Steves' Paris 2010 as well as fictional works.

Here are some web sites to get you started:
