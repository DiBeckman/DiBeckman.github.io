Kate and Anna McGarrigle
 Complainte pour Ste-Catherine
Album: The French Album
(paroles: P. Tatartcheff/musique: A. McGarrigle/Les éditions Macbec SOCAN/Bossy Publishing SOCAN) ©1975

From the liner notes:
St. Catherine is the patron saint of single girls and the name of Montreal's main street.
This was the first song co-written with Philippe Tatartcheff, a close friend since the sixties, a native of Timmins, a mining town in northern Ontario, formerly a student of French literature at La Sorbonne, now a farmer and a poet near Dunham, Québec.
