ASSESSMENT GUIDE FOR ORAL PRESENTATIONS

1=Weak 2=Moderately Weak 3=Average 4=Moderately Strong 5=Strong

1. The topic of the presentation fulfills the requirements of the assignment, including the time limit.

2. The presentation is well organized, concise, and clear.

3. The presenter appears to be prepared for the presentation.

4. The presenter speaks in clear and grammatically correct French.

5. The presenter speaks loudly enough.

6. The presenter communicates without relying too heavily on notes.

7. The presenter maintains eye contact with the audience.

8. Visual aids and use of the board are effective.
