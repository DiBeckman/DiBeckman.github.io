A CHECKLIST OF TOPICS TO DISCUSS IN PREPARATION FOR LLL LEADERSHIP
Copyright 2006 by LLLI Leader Accreditation Department (LAD)

Use your experience and Leader resources (the LEADER’S HANDBOOK, THE WOMANLY ART OF BREASTFEEDING, the BREASTFEEDING ANSWER BOOK, pamphlets and information sheets, the material in the Leader Application Packet, your Leader publications) to explore, discuss, and/or practice the following topics and skills, relating each to the Leader Applicant's preparation to fulfill the responsibilities of an LLL Leader. Add topics and themes you think would be helpful. and contact your Leader Accreditation Department representative for additional suggestions.

The following page references may help you get started. Use them if you find them helpful. Please do not feel limited by them or obligated to use them all. Topics marked with an asterisk (*) are in addition to those in the Leader’s Handbook checklist on pages 150-52 (2003 edition).

The sources referenced in this checklist are:
Leader’s Handbook 2003 edition: LH(03)
The Womanly Art of Breastfeeding 2004 edition: WAB(04)
