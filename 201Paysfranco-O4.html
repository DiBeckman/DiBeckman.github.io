For Tuesday, March 23: Find out what the weather is like in your country today. You'll be telling your classmates about it.

One good resource: http://fr.yahoo.com. Under the line À découvrir, you'll see a heading Toute l'info. Look to the right and click on Météo to get weather around the world in French. You can also search this site for information about your country.

For Tuesday, March 30:
Make a post card about your imagined (or real!) visit to your country. On one side include drawings, photos or other graphics from the country. On the other, include a message to the class in French (Chers Amis,) with answers to the following questions:
• Name of the country and where it is located
• 3 interesting/unusual facts about your country
• What you did yesterday and what the weather was like (passé composé; imparfait)
• What you are doing today and what the weather is like (présent)
• What you will do tomorrow and what the weather (use the future proche—aller+ infinitif)

Ideally, you will visit the mountains, the beach, and the countryside, so you can use the vocabulary from Unité 3.

The presentation will be graded using the Assessment Guide for Oral Presentations. The postcard will be graded using the Writing Assistance Rubric.
